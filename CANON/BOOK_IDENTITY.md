# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 043
**Title:** Slurp or Die
**Slug:** choking-noodles
**Category:** Self-Inflicted

## The Death (One Sentence)
A competitive mukbang influencer chokes to death during an ill-advised street food eating contest at a Japanese hostel, proving that content truly is king even when it kills you.

## Tagline
"He lived for the views. He died for the content."

## Themes
1. The absurdity of turning every experience into content and performance
2. The way social media metrics replace genuine human experience
3. Competitive eating culture and the commodification of bodily danger

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 55%
- Horror: 25%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
Anyone who's witnessed influencer culture invade backpacker spaces. People who've seen travelers more concerned with documenting than experiencing. Former competitive types who cringe at their past selves.

## Comparison Titles
- *Super Size Me* meets *Final Destination*

## Content Warnings
- Graphic choking/asphyxiation
- Death during eating
- Social media addiction
- Body horror elements
